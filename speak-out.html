  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Available 24/7 for crisis support
  • Crisis Text Line: Text support for immediate help
  • SAMHSA National Helpline: Free treatment referral for substance abuse
  • Mental Health America: Resources for finding local support
  • 7 Cups: Free online therapy and emotional support
  • Local community centers: Often provide free or low-cost mental health services
